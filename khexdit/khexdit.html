Introduction

KHexdit is a small and simple viewer for binary files. I stolen the DnD support of KEdit for this editor, so he now can handle URLs with kfm.

Installation

Requirements: You have to set the environment variables QTDIR and KDEDIR first. Further you need the libraries

  • libkde 0.6.1
  • libkfm out of kfm 0.5.4
