2. Panel Options.

  • Menu Tooltips are a boxes containing a short descriptive text that appear (after a short delay) when the mouse pointer is placed over a button on the Panel or an entry in the Application Starter list. If you enable Tooltips, you can use the slider to set the delay before they appear.
  • Personal First: Choosing this makes your custom entries in the user section of the Application Starter the primary entries on the menu. (The default makes the KDE application entries primary).
  • Auto Hide Panel: With this choice the Panel is normally hidden, but reappears when you touch the side of the screen where the Panel is hidden with the mouse pointer.
  • Auto Hide Taskbar: This choice similarly auto-hides the Taskbar. It also reappears when the side of the screen where it is hidden is touched by the mouse pointer.
  • The Clock may be configured for either 24 hour or 12 hour (AM/PM) styles.
