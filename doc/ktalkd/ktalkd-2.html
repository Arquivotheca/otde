2. Supported Platforms

ktalkd has been explicitely tested on the following platforms:

  • RedHat Linux 5.0
but should compile on many others. It was compiled on S.u.S.E. Linux. I would be glad to receive comments about running ktalkd on other systems. It won't work on Solaris and AIX until I rewrite the talkd.h file.
