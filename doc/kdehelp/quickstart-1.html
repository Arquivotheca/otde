1. Introduction

This document is is a brief introduction to the K Desktop Environment. It will familiarize you with some of the most basic features of KDE

This guide is far from covering all aspects of the K Desktop or even most of them. It will only describe some of the most basic ways to accomplish a few of the most common tasks.

We will also assume that you are already familiar with at least one graphical user interface, as for example CDE(tm), Geos(tm), GEM(tm), NeXT(tm), MacFinder(tm), Presentation Manager(tm) or MS-Windows(tm). So we will not explain the usage of a mouse or the single keys of your keyboard but concentrate on hopefully more interesting things.
